About

I am a robotics algorithm engineer specializing in reinforcement learning for humanoid robots and whole-body control. My work focuses on developing robust locomotion and dynamic motion policies — including agile behaviors such as jumping — and transferring them from simulation to real hardware.

Research Interests

  • Robot Reinforcement Learning RL-based locomotion for legged and humanoid robots.
  • Whole-Body Control Unified loco-manipulation; real-time whole-body motion control.
  • Sim-to-Real Transfer Domain randomization and deployment pipelines for real robotic hardware.
